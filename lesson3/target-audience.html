We the People

Target Audience

The audience is primarily the people of the town and potential visitors. This could mean anyone from town officials to local farmers, to soccer moms. Everyone in the town who is looking for information about what is happening can access this website as a one-stop shop for their inqueries.

Personas

Julian recently bought and outfitted a campervan. He and his girlfriend are travelling the continential US. They mostly use hotspot on their laptops to connect to the wifi when they can't find an internet cafe. They are digital nomads. Julian is a software engineer. He tries to avoid all mainstream franchises while travelling opting instead for local businesses. He frequently googles the towns that they pass through looking for things to do and place to eat that will enrich his nomadic lifestyle.

Dmitriy is a stay-at-home mom of three kids, all under the age of seven. Her and her husband live on a hobby farm. She busies herself by teaching the kids things like gardening, fetching eggs from the chickens, and cleaning. Since they are outside often, they regularly check the weather. Additionally, Dmitriy is likes to be involved with the community and give back whenever she can. She would access this site as she looks for coummunity events on her desktop computer or her smart phone.
